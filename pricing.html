Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I view the plans?

You can find details of our plans and their prices on the pricing page. 

If you still have questions, feel free to contact us.

Can I upgrade my plan?

Yes. If you have reached the limit for Dynamic QR Codes or would like to unlock more features, you can upgrade to a higher plan anytime.

However, while upgrading, you cannot switch from yearly billing to monthly billing. 

Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?

No, there are no set up fees or hidden costs.

Which currencies do you support?

We currently accept payments in US dollars only. You can pay by credit or debit cards or Paypal.

Will I get a price adjustment if I upgrade my plan?

Yes. During your account upgrade, the previous unused credit is adjusted automatically according to the remaining period of your previous plan.

However, while upgrading, you cannot switch from yearly billing to monthly billing. 

Is there any refund policy?

Yes, for your first-time purchase, you may ask for a refund anytime within 15 days from the date of purchase, and you will receive a full refund.

Just drop an email to our support team and provide your reason for the refund, so that we may improve our services based on your feedback.

Can I pay via Paypal?

Yes, you will have the option on the checkout to pay via Paypal. 

You may also directly pay to payments@qrcodechimp.com, and send our support team an email with your account and transaction details. We will update your account to the right package.

How can I cancel my subscription renewal?

Please contact our support team at support@qrcodechimp.com at least a day before your renewal date, for the cancellation. 
Please clearly mention your account email id and the reason for the cancellation.

What is the difference between basic statistics and advanced statistics?

In Basic Analytics we show total Scan Counts and few other data.

In Advanced Analytics we provide you everything in Basic Analytics plus:
- Timeline Analysis (Day wise data)
- Top Performing Tags 
- Top Locations
- Hour-Wise Analysis
- Day-Wise Analysis
- Device Analysis
- Browser Analysis
- Geo Analysis (Map-based view)
- Excel download of the data for you to do further detailed analytics.

What does 1000 scans per month mean?

Under the free plan, we provide 1000 scans per month free for all the dynamic codes combined. Once you reach the 1000 scans limit for a month, scans are paused, but they reopen next month for another 1000 scans.

What is the validity of my QR code?

QR code will be valid forever. If it's dynamic, it will be paused if their scan limit for the month is reached.

If you are a paid user, the dynamic QR codes given in your plan will remain active till you recharge the plan regularly. Otherwise, your account will be moved to a free plan, and the dynamic codes other than the last 10 will be paused.

We have free as well as paid plans. You may explore our plans here - https://www.qrcodechimp.com/pricing
